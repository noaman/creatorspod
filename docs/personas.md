Creators Pod – Persona Framework
Structure Overview
For each persona, we'll follow this format:

Persona Name & Archetype
One-liner description
Product mapping (deck / feature hooks): agent name, KPIs, modalities, channels, second brain hook, trust/approvals, intensity anchor
Three core pain points
Workflow stages (Ideation → Creation → Publishing → Analytics)
Pain-to-product mapping (per stage: what they do, pain, platform delivers, powered by)


1. Growth Scout
Content Creator / Influencer
Who they are
A hybrid creator publishing both filmed content and AI-generated derivatives across short- and long-form channels, focused on sustainable growth velocity.

**Product mapping (deck / feature hooks)**  
- **Agent:** Growth Scout  
- **KPIs:** Follower & watch-time growth, engagement rate, sponsorship-readiness (consistent cadence + brand-safe output)  
- **Modalities:** Video (primary), images, text, audio clips, lightweight interactive (polls / CTAs)  
- **Channels:** YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, X, Pinterest, personal blog  
- **Second brain hook:** Trend signals + past performance inform the Daily Brief and cut topic paralysis  
- **Trust / approvals:** Human final say on hooks and sensitive topics; platform-specific policy checks before publish  
- **Intensity anchor (illustrative):** High volume of formats and derivatives per week; platform aims to shrink planning/packaging time sharply vs. manual toolchains  

Three core pain points

Topic paralysis – Spending hours deciding what to post, constantly second-guessing relevance and trend-fit
Production bottleneck – Drafting, formatting, and packaging content for multiple platforms eats up creative time
Blind optimization – No clear feedback loop connecting what works to what gets made next

Workflow stages
Ideation
What they do: Scan trends, brainstorm angles, assess what fits their niche
Pain: Too many signals, unclear which topics will resonate, starting from scratch daily
Platform delivers: Daily Brief with ranked topics, personalized fit scores, suggested angles grounded in memory
Powered by: Trend Monitor + Second Brain
Creation
What they do: Write hooks, script content, design thumbnails, draft captions for each platform
Pain: Every piece needs manual adaptation; hard to maintain voice consistency across formats
Platform delivers: Response pack with hook variants, script drafts, thumbnail prompts, platform-specific captions
Powered by: Viral Trend Response + Voice DNA + Studio
Publishing
What they do: Format assets per channel, schedule posts, ensure metadata and policy compliance
Pain: Fragmented tools, manual scheduling, last-minute format fixes
Platform delivers: Channel-specific variants with policy checks, unified approval queue, best-time scheduling
Powered by: Publishing Scheduler + Brand Voice Enforcer + Orchestrator
Analytics
What they do: Check engagement, identify spikes, decide what to double down on
Pain: Data scattered across platforms; insights arrive too late to act on
Platform delivers: Live alerts on anomalies, end-of-day performance summaries, learning loop updates for tomorrow's brief
Powered by: Unified Analytics + Alert Agents + Performance Agents + Learning Loop
