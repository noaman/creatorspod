04 / 25

The product

Your AI second brain

Creators Pod is a persistent, goal-driven co-pilot: it stores everything in a personal knowledge graph, reasons over performance, and executes publishing workflows — instead of waiting for the next prompt.

  • Remembers — drafts, approvals, analytics, brand rules, audience notes, sources. No more "start from zero" every session.
  • Acts proactively — trend scans, scheduled generation, alerts when content over- or under-performs versus baseline.
  • Learns continuously — every published asset's outcome updates modality mix, hooks, and cadence recommendations.
  • Orchestrates delivery — six content types, auto-adapted and policy-checked per destination, with approval gates you control.
Assistive vs. agentic: Typical stacks generate text on demand but don't remember the creator, learn from performance, schedule across platforms, or act proactively. Creators Pod does all four in one continuous loop.
  • Compliance-aware generation — disclosure, copyright, and regulatory flags embedded for regulated teams and publishers.
  • Persona-tuned agents — same engine, different surface: Growth Scout vs. Authority Builder vs. Campaign Orchestrator.
